About Us

At Raven's Intelligence, we are passionate about transforming education in the Caribbean through innovative technology. Our team comprises educators, AI experts, and software developers dedicated to enhancing the learning experience for students. We understand the unique challenges faced by students in the Caribbean, particularly in STEM subjects, and are committed to providing tailored, interactive, and effective learning solutions. Through our Tutoring AI Assistants, we aim to empower students by offering personalized support, enabling them to thrive academically.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a future where every student in the Caribbean has access to high-quality, engaging, and interactive educational resources. We believe that technology can bridge the gap in traditional learning methods, fostering a generation of confident learners equipped with the skills needed to excel in STEM fields. By harnessing the power of AI, we aim to revolutionize education and inspire students to reach their full potential.
